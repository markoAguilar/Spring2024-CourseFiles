Funtown, USA

Location

Find this oh so serene and verdant location of Funtown only 1.12 million light years from Earth
in the paradise planet Kepler 62 E!

About

Weather

The weather is completely serene, with little to no storms. And enough consistent light drizzles
to healthily sustain the local flora and fauna!

Fauna

Get to meet all the crazy new life forms here on Kepler 62 E. Most of them are friendly and pose no
immediate threat to the human race!

Flora

You ever watched the movie Avatar? Well the plant life here is just like that! It's like a dream come true!

Things To Do

  • Go on a tour in the local jungles to harvest and consume the exotic edible plant life!
